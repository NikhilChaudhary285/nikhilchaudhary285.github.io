üöß Multiplayer Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Ensuring all players start the match simultaneously
    Solution: Implemented a player-ready system using Photon room properties, combined with PhotonNetwork.AutomaticallySyncScene.
  • Challenge: Supporting multiple tank types and weapons without hardcoding
    Solution: Used ScriptableObjects to define tanks, weapons, stats, and upgrades.
  • Challenge: Maintaining clean gameplay flow in a networked environment
    Solution: Structured gameplay using event-driven systems and coroutines instead of heavy Update() logic.
